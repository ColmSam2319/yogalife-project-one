About Us

Yogalife has been a renowned Athlone area Yoga Studio since 2010. At Yogalife we bring a unique approach to delivering our classes Our yoga classes embody physical, spiritual, and emotional aspects of wellbeing. Instructors will offer exercise variations that suit beginners and advanced members. Come on a journey with us and let us show you the way to your best self.

we offer

  • Beginner Yoga- classes that will allow new members to join with us in a 30 minute introductory class
  • Advanced yoga - class for the seasoned pro
  • Senior yoga - yoga for the older adult
  • junior yoga - yoga for kids
  • rehab yoga - yoga for injury prevention and rehabilitiation
  • new mum yoga - yoga for mums of new arrivals
  • Tecnique classes - learn to master moves
