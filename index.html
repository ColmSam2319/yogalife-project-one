About Us

Who are we?

Yogalife studios have been at the forefront of studio classes for the last twenty years. With our highly quilified instructors we offer the best yoga classes in the Midlands. Our studios are purpose build to offer the best yoga experience to you as well as offering extensive classes through out the week. Yogalife offers classes for all type of users be it a beginner to a yoga pro Yogalife has a choice for you.

Your Instructors

Our Yogalife instructors have over 100 years teaching experience between them. Each one of our instructors are highly qualified all staff have completed the Himalayan Yoga Institutes a 200-hour yoga retreat. this has given our instructors the rank of Ashtanga Master.

Yogalife Classes

Beginner Yoga

Join our instructor for a 30 minute introductory class that will teach you the moves and the structure of a class.

Tecnique classes

This 45 minute class focus on the correct tecnique as well as breathing excercises that will be used in our classes.

Yoga Classes

A hour long yoga class with ten minutes of guided meditation at the end perfect to unwind.

Pro Yoga

Using advance yoga poses that challenge you the hour long class will ensure that you feel energised and refreshed.

Rehab Yoga

Designed with rehabilitiation in mind these classes allow you to improve mobility while strenthening injured muscles.

Postnatal Yoga

This form of yoga focuses on recovery and restoration and can alleviate stress and depression after giving birth.
